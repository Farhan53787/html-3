Codingal

Codingal is a leading online platform providing live, instructor-led coding and AI classes for children and teens aged 5-18 (K-12). It offers a STEM.org accredited, project-based curriculum that includes subjects like Python, web development, app creation, and AI, aiming to prepare students for a digital future.
